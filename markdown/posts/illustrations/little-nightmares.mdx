Little Nightmares is a puzzle-platform horror adventure game developed by
Tarsier Studios and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment for PlayStation 4,
Windows and Xbox One. A Nintendo Switch version was released in May 2018,
followed by a Google Stadia version in June 2020 and then a mobile version will
be released in winter 2023 and published by Playdigious. Set in a mysterious
world, Little Nightmares follows the journey of Six, a hungry little girl who
must escape the Maw, an iron vessel inhabited by monstrous, twisted beings.
