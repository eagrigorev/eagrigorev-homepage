This is a series of illustration based on a traditional Japanese technique of
[woodblock printing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Woodblock_printing_in_Japan).
This technique was videly used by
[Kawase Hasui](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hasui_Kawase),
[Hokusai](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hokusai) and others. For this series I
used the
["Japanese Woodblock"](https://lospec.com/palette-list/japanese-woodblock)
palette by [Polyducks](https://polyducks.co.uk/).

<SpacerParagraph />

Most of the pictures have real life woodblock references.
